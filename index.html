How to Play:

You and your opponent are the only human controlled characters on the screen.

The rest of the characters are decoys, and move in a very predictable pattern. You will want to imitate their movements as best you can.

Your goal is to find and destroy your opponent, while maintaining your anonymity.

Controls:

Player One moves with the WASD keys, and shoots with SPACE.

Player Two moves with the ARROW keys, and shoots with ENTER.

Parting Words:

Your placement at the beginning of the game is random, so look closely to find yourself, and then look even closer to find your opponent.

Good Luck!
